Neon lights

Neon lights are iconic, vibrant light sources often used for advertising and artistic displays. Created by passing an electric current through neon gas, these lights emit a distinctive bright glow in various colors, depending on the gas and coating used. First discovered in the early 20th century, neon lights quickly became synonymous with nightlife and urban culture, adding a dynamic, colorful element to street scapes, signs, and creative installations around the world.
